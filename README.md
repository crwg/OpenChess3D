No ads. No paywalls. No subscriptions. Just chess, the way it should be.

## 🌟 Our Promise

- **Forever free** – You will never be asked to pay for features or content.
- **Open source** – Anyone can inspect, modify, and redistribute the code under the GPLv3 license.
- **Community driven** – Your feedback and contributions shape the future of OpenChess3D.
- **Respect for creators** – We give full credit to the authors of the Lozza engine and all other dependencies.

## ✨ Features

- 🏆 **Play vs Strong AI** – Challenge [Lozza](https://github.com/op12no2/lozza), a powerful open‑source UCI engine.
- 📜 **PGN Import / Export** – Analyze your games or share them with friends.
- 🎮 **Immersive 3D Board** – Rotate, zoom, and enjoy realistic pieces with dynamic lighting.
- 🎚️ **Adjustable Difficulty** – From beginner to grandmaster, the AI adapts to your level.
- 🧩 **Open & Extensible** – Built with modern C++ and a clean architecture.
